MD9 Tool Help

1. Open files
   - Open individual files or a whole folder.
   - You can drop md9, ani, obj, glb, gltf, bin, and common texture files.

2. View
   - Left mouse button rotates, mouse wheel zooms, right mouse button pans.
   - The panel can toggle textures, wireframe, skeleton, normals, bounds, and grid.

3. Edit parts
   - Click Edit in the part list.
   - You can change parent, material, position, rotation, and scale.
   - You can replace the mesh with OBJ / GLB / GLTF.
   - You can restore, clear mesh, or delete a part.

4. Export
   - Export on a part row exports that part as GLB.
   - Export selected exports all checked parts as one GLB.

5. Save MD9
   - New textures are downloaded first.
   - Then enter the texture filename that should be written into the MD9.
   - Finally the edited MD9 is downloaded.
